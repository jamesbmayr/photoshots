PhotoShots

About

PhotoShots is photography hide and seek - phone tag reimagined.

With 5 different game modes, you and some friends can transform photography into a competitive sport.

Setup

  • Each player creates an account with a unique username.
  • Your username becomes a QR code - print that out, twice, and tape it to your front and back.
  • Find a place where you can run and hide - someplace with obstacles and multiple routes.
  • When everybody's in the game, you'll get 10 seconds to spread out before the photoshooting begins.
  • Players get random targets. If you capture yours, they're out, and you take their target.
  • Anyone out can get back in... by capturing someone still in the game.

Game Modes

  • 15 minutes of fame - It's 15 minutes of mayhem to take as many shots as you can.
  • Last One Standing - When only one player is still in it, they win it.
  • 10 min in - Win by staying in for 10 minutes, minus any time you're out.
  • str8 shooter - If you capture 8 times more than you get captured, you win.
  • one stun done - When all others are captured, you win. If another survives, most shots wins.
